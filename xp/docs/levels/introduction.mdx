# Levels in Level Up XP

Levels in XP are **milestones that learners reach as they accumulate points**. Each level represents a stage in their journey through your course or training, providing a clear sense of progress and achievement.

Levels are:

- **Based on points**: A learner's total points determine their current level.
- **Defined by thresholds**: Each level has a starting number of points, and a length (how many more points are needed to reach the next level).
- **Visible to learners**: Learners can see their current level and what they need to do to reach the next one.

Levels help you:

- **Make progress visible**: Learners can see how far they have come and how far they have to go.
- **Motivate ongoing engagement**: Reaching a new level feels like an achievement and encourages learners to keep participating.
- **Structure your gamification**: You can align levels with key milestones in your course, such as completing modules or mastering skills.
