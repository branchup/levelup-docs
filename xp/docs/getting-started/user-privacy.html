User information and privacy

The information collected by the Moodle plugin Level Up XP is contained within the Moodle installation and is never transmitted, communicated or shared with us or third parties. The mechanisms for attributing points to learners, generating the leaderboard, sending notifications, etc... is executed within Moodle itself.

As of Moodle 3.3.5, 3.4.2, 3.5 or greater, Level Up XP is conforming to Moodle's requirements for GDPR compliance.
